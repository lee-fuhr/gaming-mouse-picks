Best for specific needs

Best for competitive fps

For twitch‑aiming and fast flicks, the Logitech G Pro X Superlight 2’s low weight and flawless sensor give you an edge. Its universal shape works for claw and fingertip grips common in shooters, and the wireless performance is rock‑solid even during intense matches. Our pick: Logitech G Pro X Superlight 2.

Best for all‑day comfort

If you play long sessions or prioritize hand comfort, the Razer DeathAdder V3 Pro’s sculpted right‑hand shape cradles your palm without causing fatigue. Its light weight and top‑tier sensor keep performance high even after hours of use. Our pick: Razer DeathAdder V3 Pro.

Best for budget wireless

The Logitech G305 Lightspeed delivers reliable low‑latency wireless at a very accessible price. It’s an ideal choice for new gamers, students, or anyone who wants a solid wireless mouse without spending a lot. Our pick: Logitech G305 Lightspeed.

Our verdict

Whether you’re grinding ranked matches or just want a more responsive click, the right gaming mouse makes a real difference. The Logitech G Pro X Superlight 2 remains our top recommendation for its balanced blend of weight, performance, and battery life. That said, the Razer DeathAdder V3 Pro is a superb alternative if ergonomics are your priority, and the Logitech G305 proves you can get excellent wireless on a tighter budget. Consider your hand size, grip style, and must‑have features, then pick the one that feels like an extension of your hand.
